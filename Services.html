Use Case #2

Vehicle Control Module

The Forklift Control module monitors critical safety factors including:

  • Forklift and pedestrian sharing the same aisle
  • Maintaining safe forklift proximity
  • Detection of incorrect forklift parking
  • Monitoring Truck Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

Use Case #3

Area Controls Module

This module enhances workplace safety by tracking personnel behavior and enforcing restricted area policies:

  • Monitoring of restricted or no-go zones
  • Tracking operator time spent in specific zones
  • Detection of workers under suspended loads
  • Machine proximity monitoring
  • Maximum worker count enforcement
  • Minimum staffing level detection

Use Case #4

Emergency Response Module

Designed to assist in critical situations, this module provides real-time monitoring and alerts during emergencies:

  • Detection and count of individuals in emergency zones
  • Identification of workers down or unresponsive
  • Monitoring for overcrowded areas
  • Alerting during total power or lighting failures
  • Detection of improper use of emergency exits

Use Case #5

Housekeeping Compliance Module

This module ensures day-to-day cleanliness and obstruction-free environments to support safety and compliance:

  • Verification that emergency exits are kept clear
  • Detection of blocked walkways or pathways
  • Monitoring for oil, water, or chemical spills on floors

Use Case #6

General Safety Alerts Module

This module addresses a range of additional safety concerns, helping enforce safe practices across the facility:

  • Detection of machine guard presence at key points
  • Alerts for tampered or non-functioning cameras
  • Monitoring of stair use without handrail support
  • Open or unsecured electrical panels
